A Ten Year Assessment of Our Hosting Options
Background

CarrickDojo opened it's doors in January 2015 and has been a great success ever since. The website for the Dojo was setup at the end of 2014 and this year it will be running for ten years.

Our Wordpress site and GMail address have served us well over the years. Our Dojo champions have spent many hours keeping the website up-to date and integrating new functionality like our ticketing solution.

With the newly developed ticketing system from the CoderDojo along with a host of changes to data and child protection legislation in Ireland over the decade, now seems to good time to do a complete assessment of the systems we use to keep our Dojo running.

Goals
  • Ensure best practices with a child-first led approach to data and accessibility for all Dojo digital resources.
  • Ensure best value of Dojo resources is being met with regard to digital services.
  • Assess the use of social media for operational and promotional purposes.
  • Ensure adequate data retention and information lifecycle policies are in place for methods of communicating with the Dojo
  • Assess options to redevelop the website in HTML and CSS to better align with the Raspberry Pi Web Design projects.
  • Document the website development and maintenance from first-principles with the aim of Ninja's being able to get further involved.
