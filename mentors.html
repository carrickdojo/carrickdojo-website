To cater for our group in Carrick On Shannon, we are looking for extra mentors to join our team of volunteers. It’s a great opportunity to get involved in a fun, creative, and worthwhile youth movement.

Want to share your coding talents? CoderDojo is a free computer club to teach young people how to code and we’re always on the lookout for talented coding volunteers. At a CoderDojo, young people learn how to write code, develop websites, apps, games and more. Dojos are setup, run by and taught by volunteers. All this is completely free to participants.

We are looking for admin mentors too just to help out with managing the kids and helping with setup etc..

If you have any coding experience or other skills that would be useful to CoderDojo, and would like to apply to become a mentor, please complete this form.
